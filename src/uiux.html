Metro OS Marketplace


The Metro App Store is a brand-new marketplace designed for an open-source project, aiming to provide a seamless, secure, and user-centric experience for discovering and managing applications. Unlike traditional proprietary app stores, Metro is built with transparency, accessibility, and community-driven innovation at its core. The store is designed to support developers and users within the open-source ecosystem, fostering a collaborative space for software distribution.

This project is a deliberate attempt to revive the dying Metro design language—known for its minimalist, typography-centric, and tile-based aesthetics—by integrating its classic principles with modern design techniques. By reintroducing these elements, the design not only enhances app discovery and usability but also pays homage to a design language that once defined a generation of mobile experiences.

Healthy Paws


The wireframes depict Healthy Paws, a pet health management app designed to streamline medication tracking and pet care.


Key Features:


Pet Profiles: Stores details like breed, medications, vaccinations, and notes for multiple pets.


Pill Manager:

Schedules medication reminders with dosage details (e.g., 2×10g pills at 11:15 AM).

Tracks "Taken" or "Missed" doses and logs side effects (abdominal pain, diarrhea).

Supports multi-caretaker collaboration.


Notifications: Sends lock screen alerts to ensure timely administration.


Settings: Customizes notifications, caretaker details, date/time formats, language, and accessibility.


Navigation: Bottom tabs for switching between Pill Manager, Pet Profiles, and Settings.


Purpose: Simplifies pet healthcare by centralizing medical data, reminders, and caretaker coordination in an intuitive interface.
