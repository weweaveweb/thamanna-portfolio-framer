Based in Sheffield, UK, Im a versatile artist with an eye for policy analysis. I carry four years of accomplished history in areas such as graphic design, branding, and crafting digital strategies, with a focus on developing audience-centric visual narratives. Together with my thorough understanding of economic development and policy scrutiny, my creative pursuits acquire a unique blend of artistic vision and analytical reasoning.
In my spare time, I explore the realm of the arts producing portraits, generating mixed-media embellishments, discovering novel techniques, and encapsulating transitory instants via the lens of a camera. A relentless explorer and a museum aficionado, I gather my inspiration from varied cultures, bustling markets, and unnoticed elegance found in daily life. My deliverables, be it an analytical paper, a social campaign, or a canvas teeming with raw sentiments, marries discipline and impulsiveness.
Off duty, I can be found unearthing the concealed treasures of England, assembling Spotify playlists imbued with the pulsating rhythms of Bollywood, or in search of the crunchiest panipuri. My ever-increasing assortment of IKEA mugs and penchant for seasonal lattes reflect my belief that contentment resides in minutiae.
Why not join me over a discussion on design, policy, or the enchantment involved in transforming notions into manifest creations?
