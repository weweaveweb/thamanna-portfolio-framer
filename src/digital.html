#TextureTuesday


I developed the Texture Tuesday series a weekly social media post showcasing rich and dynamic interior textures. The goal was to highlight the role of textures in elevating interior spaces, from soft fabrics to raw materials, helping the audience visualize depth and contrast in design. Each post follows a refined, aesthetically cohesive layout, ensuring consistency with Bonito Designs' branding.

#MoodboardMonday


I created the Moodboard Monday series, a weekly social media post featuring curated interior design mood boards. The goal was to inspire the audience with carefully selected color palettes, textures, and design elements that align with Bonito Designs' aesthetic. Each post maintains a clean, visually engaging layout, ensuring a balance between creativity and brand consistency.
